Seat Reservation

To book a seat, start by selecting your preferred event from the main dashboard. Once you've chosen the event, you can proceed to reserve a seat using the provided seat chart by clicking 'reserve.' Please note that the system allows only one reservation per person, meaning each seat is linked to a single account. Be sure to choose your ideal spot!

Already reserved?

The system policy allows each account to reserve only one seat per event. If you encounter an issue, it may be related to your previous reservations. You can check your account to see if a reservation has already been made for the event.

Attending a paid event?

Simply follow these steps for payment:
Reserve a seat -> Timer Starts
After making a reservation, a timer will begin for the payment deadline. You are required to pay within the specified time-frame. If the payment is not completed, the seat will only be partially held and may be released.
Pay upfront -> Cashier will verify -> Get the OTP code -> Enter the OTP code
After entering the OTP code, the seat will be completely reserved and your ticket will be sent in your email
